About us

Based at the Institute of Psychological Research in Córdoba, Argentina, our interdisciplinary team studies the mind and the brain, seeking to understand how different modes of experience and behavior emerge. Our research focuses on three main areas: states of consciousness, metacognition—the capacity to reflect on one’s own decisions—and the mechanisms that sustain attention.

We combine psychophysical experiments, EEG, and fMRI with computational models, linking fundamental questions with clinical applications such as prognosis in disorders of consciousness and the early detection of neurodegenerative processes.
