Cognitive Science Group
click para español


We are based at the Institute of Psychological Research (CONICET–UNC) in Córdoba, Argentina.

We study how internal brain states shape experience, perception, and behavior, combining psychophysical and behavioral experiments, neuroimaging (EEG, fMRI), and computational modeling across multiple levels, from large-scale brain dynamics to subjective reports.

Part of this research extends to collaborative clinical and translational contexts, including prognosis in disorders of consciousness and studies related to neurodegenerative conditions.
